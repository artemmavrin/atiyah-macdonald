In a Boolean ring \(A\), show that
\begin{parts}
\part
\(2x = 0\) for all \(x\in A\);
\part
every prime ideal \(\mathfrak p\) is maximal, and \(A/\mathfrak p\) is a field with two elements;
\part
every finitely generated ideal in \(A\) is principal.
\end{parts}
